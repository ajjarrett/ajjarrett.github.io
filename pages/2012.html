The Olympic Games

The Olympic Games present an opportunity for the world to come together and enjoy athleticism and sportsmanship from the world's greatest athletes as they compete in a variety of athletic events. The Olympic Games take place every four years at a different host city from around the world. Since 1994, the games have alternated between the Summer and Winter Olympics every two years.
